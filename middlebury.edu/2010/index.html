You’ve got four years to try new ideas, to explore the subjects and pursuits you feel most passionate about. You’ve got four years to explore a curriculum so diverse and interdisciplinary that it would take a century to experience it all.

But in those same four years you might act in a student production, do research with faculty scientists, join a relief mission to a country halfway around the world, sing a capella, hike the Long Trail, or play left wing on the hockey team.

We can’t tell you where you’ll wind up. Our job is to help you get started.
